About Us

CampusBiz has constantly been working with industry experts, entrepreneurs, and investors to form a network which it calls The Indiprenuer network, as well as student bodies in colleges to come up up with a totaly innovative method of empowering students in colleges. The team has worked carefully for over a year to design a program that helps develop the core skill sets for students at their time in college and help them idntify there true attributes. Also, CampusBiz helps bridge the gap between companies/ startups to help engage with each other.

Services

CampusBiz helps students get real time work experience in the form of tasks, projects, internships & fellowhips or developing your own business with the aim of equiping yourself for the real world challenges.
